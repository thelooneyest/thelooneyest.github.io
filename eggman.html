Eggman/Spark Central

Project created with:

  • Illustrator
  • InDesign
  • Photoshop

This project was made in partnership with Spark Central in Spokane, WA. Spark Central was in need of promotional material for upcoming events that could be used in online ads and social media posts.

The look of the character was meant to be playful and takes inspiration from the term "egghead" which means someone who enjoys learning/studying. Since the events were all for learning/teaching events, I felt this was a fun way to portray the idea. The colors used are those that were provided in the Spark Central company style guide.
