Scentco Fundraising website

Project created with:

  • WordPress
  • Bootstrap
  • HTML
  • CSS

This project was created for Scentco Inc., a company specializing in scented and unscented toys and school supplies. The custom theme was created by the designer who worked at the company before I started my position. My main job was to create any new custom page layouts, add new products, and keep the site up to date and functioning properly.

During my time at the company, I created various custom pages, landing pages, informational pages, online forms, and downloadable forms. The majority of the projects were created with Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS.
