Pot Pantry Packaging Design

Project created with:

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator

This project was a logo and branding project for a fictional line of cannabis-infused cooking products. Research was done into existing legal cannabis brand packaging, as well as modern cooking ingredient brands. I created the logo, branding, colors and packaging design including physical mock-ups.
