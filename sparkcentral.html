Spark Central Partnership Programs

Project created with:

  • Photoshop
  • Corel Painter

This project was created for InMotion USA for their Solowheel Electric Unicycle products. The flyers were made to hand out to attendees at the San Diego Comic-Con 2018.

The look of the flyers was meant to be fun and colorful and bring in the idea of superheroes through the treatment of the 'Solowheel' text ast the title. The background image is the skyline of the city of San Diego.
