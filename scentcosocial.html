Scentco Inc. Social Media Posts

Project created with:

  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop

These images were created for the Scentco Inc. Instagram account. The posts are meant to be fun and light-hearted and appeal to children and parents who would be purchasing products for children.

Images were created mostly in Adobe Illustrator with Photoshop used to add in any photos of physical products for the post. These posts also utilized Instagram shopping integration to add product links to the photos to encourage customer interaction to learn more about products that are either featured in the post or related to the content of the post.
