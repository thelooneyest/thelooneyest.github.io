Maxwell Fashions was started by the fashion creed, “Modern Fun Meets Timeless Sophistication”.

Established in 2013 with custom evening dresses. "If you didn't have a place to go, when you see my dress you'll find a place to go!" -MAXWELL

In 2014 MAXACTIVE was born launching basics, starting exclusively with tank tops made from bamboo.

We are a young company with goals of launching a new staple garment to our ready-to-wear line every few months, as we continuously learn and perfect the process of mass production. Subscribe to our mailing list and stay up to date with what's new at Maxwell Fashions!
