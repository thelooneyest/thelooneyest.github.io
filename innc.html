INN Connection website contest

Project created with:

  • Photoshop
  • Sketch

This project was created for Inland Northwest Nature Connection. This organization partners with different schools and organizations to educate students about nature and the outdoors in the Inland Northwest area.

INNC wanted a website that would be beneficial to both students and to other organizations that wanted to partner with them. This design provided an easy to navigate one-page design that provided simple and clear information. Throughout the page were different ways to prompt the viewer to donate to encourage support of the INNC program.
