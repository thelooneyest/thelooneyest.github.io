Porky's Donuts

Project created with:

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • InDesign

This project was a logo and branding project for a fictional donut shop. I created the logo, decided on colors, and did mock-ups for the product packaging and work uniforms.

Donuts are something that people of all ages enjoy, but they are never seen as anything too serious, so I made the logo and colors very light-hearted.
